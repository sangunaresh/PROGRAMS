simple receipe

An omelet is a dish you might order for breakfast or brunch it's kind of like a folded pancake made of beaten eggs, sometimes with the addition of vegetables, cheese, or meat.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meat,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl beat the eggs with pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: place a non-stick frying pan over a medium flame and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omlette: once butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan evenly eggs to coat surface.
  4. Add fillings: When the eggs bgin to set at edges sprinkle your needs over omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omlette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge fold it over spillings,let it cook for one more minute,then slide it on to plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if nedeed.
